Hobbies and Personal Interests

I did this by communicating with my allocated students through email and in person weekly, addressing any concerns or issues they may have had.
    • I was able to develop teamwork and communication skills through producing podcasts with my fellow school mentors tackling problems such as toxic masculinity and maintaining a healthy school-life balance.
